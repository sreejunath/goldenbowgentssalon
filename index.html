About Us

Located in the heart of Sharjah - Abu Shagara, Golden Bow Gents Salon offers a wide range of grooming services with professional care and comfort. We pride ourselves on hygiene, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Our team of experienced barbers and stylists are dedicated to providing a personalized grooming experience for every client. From classic haircuts and precise shaves to relaxing head massages and rejuvenating facials, we use only high-quality products to ensure you look and feel your best. Walk in or book an appointment for a premium salon experience designed for the modern gentleman.
